# Data Retention Policy

Version: 2.1
Effective: 2025-01-01
Supersedes: Data Retention Policy v2.0 (2023-06-15)

## Scope

This policy applies to all personal data processed by the organization,
including employee data, customer data, and third-party data.

## Requirements

1. Personal data must not be retained longer than necessary for the
   purpose for which it was collected
2. Retention periods must be documented for each data category
3. Automated deletion must be implemented for data past its retention period
4. Legal hold requests override standard retention periods

## Retention periods

| Data category | Retention period | Legal basis |
|--------------|-----------------|-------------|
| Customer transaction records | 7 years | Tax regulations |
| Employee HR records | Duration of employment + 6 years | Labor law |
| Marketing consent records | Until consent withdrawn + 1 year | GDPR Art. 7 |
| System access logs | 90 days | Security policy |

## Compliance

- Annual audit of retention period adherence (Q1)
- Non-compliance reported to the Data Protection Officer within 72 hours
- Violations may result in disciplinary action per the employee handbook
