# Spaced repetition and writing

Reading about spaced repetition today. The idea that you strengthen memory
by revisiting material at increasing intervals.

What if the same principle applies to writing? Revisiting and rewriting
notes at intervals could deepen understanding, not just recall.

Luhmann's slip-box method involves revisiting notes when making connections.
That's a form of spaced repetition applied to thinking, not just memory.
